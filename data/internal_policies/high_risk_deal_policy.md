# High-Risk Deal Policy

A deal should be treated as high risk if one or more of the following conditions are present:

- The customer health score is below 50.
- The deal probability is below 0.40.
- The expected close date is less than 30 days away.
- The customer has unresolved implementation or onboarding concerns.
- There has been no recent customer contact.

Recommended actions for high-risk deals:

1. Schedule a follow-up with the customer within five business days.
2. Prepare a written summary of the main risks.
3. Involve the account manager and customer success team.
4. Send missing documentation or implementation materials.
5. Update the sales pipeline record after the follow-up.
